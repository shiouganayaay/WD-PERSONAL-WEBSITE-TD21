OVERVIEW

Art has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember, it’s something that I genuinely feel like I cannot live without. I’m also more used to drowning myself in doing it than not, because I’m so used to having done it in every part of my life wherever I may be. Art can mean a lot to me, from something that comforts me, to one of the things that I can easily lose my mind on the most, but really, it’s one of my biggest escapes on reality.

I can never imagine myself living a life pursuing anything else that isn’t related to art in someway, so I’ve decided to dedicate myself in learning it hence why I chose multimedia as my course. Here are some of the personal and projects I’ve done in the past few months, these are either hobby art or projects from school.

In addition I’d like to share three of my biggest inspirations in art, those whom I strongly follow and aspire to be as skillful as.

Jean-Michel Basquiat

Kim Jung Gi

Yusuke Murata
