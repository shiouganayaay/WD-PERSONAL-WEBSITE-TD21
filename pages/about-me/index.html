News Paper Layout

During my 2 years in Senior High School,
I became the lead layout artist for our School Newspaper. Throughout my whole experience,
I learned that the functionality of a design is extremely relevant and proper

Backdrop Designer

Both on Junior High School and Senior High School
I was assigned to be one of the head artist for our Intramural backdrop artworks on
the specific years (2019 and 2022), the experience thought me how to handle
big teams as well as communicate more efficiently to get points and
suggestions across to all the members involved in the project.

Tshirt Designer

In my first year of college I was given the opportunity
